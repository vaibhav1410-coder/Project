Prevention

The test will be carried out with following major prevention steps.

Person taking sample will wear a PPE kit.
Person is not allowed without token.
Person having token number less than other person's token number will be allowed first(e.g. if person A have token 1 and person B have token 2 then A will be allowed first).
Person can come to test center at any time according to his/her alloted token number(e.g. if person C have token 12 then he can come at 12x5=60 minutes late from starting time of test).
