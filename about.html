About this website

Coronavirus disease(Covid-19) is an infectious disease caused by a newly discovered coronavirus.Most people infected with Covid -19 virus will experience mild to moderate respiratory illness and recover without requiring special treatment.Older people and those with underlying medical problem like cardiovascular disease,etc. are at higher risk of death.Thus,the disease must be stopped from spreading and Covid-19 testing is the way to know about people affected with this disease.

This website is designed to provide management for Covid-19 testing.You can book a token for testing in any test center. This website is currently associated with Raj Private Lab and will add on new test centres in near future.
